# Privacy Policy — Beach Tennis Score

> Last updated: May 1, 2025

### No data collection
Beach Tennis Score does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. The app has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs.

### Match data
Match history is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's on-device storage (SwiftData). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the app developer.

### Apple Watch communication
The app communicates between your iPhone and Apple Watch using Apple's WatchConnectivity framework. This communication is local, encrypted by the operating system, and only transfers match scores between your own devices.

### iCloud
The app does not use iCloud or any other cloud storage.

### Changes to this policy
If the app ever changes in ways that affect data collection, this policy will be updated before those changes take effect.
